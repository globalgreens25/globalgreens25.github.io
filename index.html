Growing the Path to a Greener Future

Who We Are

Global Greens is a global restaurant chain featuring authentically crafted vegan dishes from all around the world. Beyond that, our foundation advocates for sustainable food practices and a greener Earth.

Our Mission

At Global Greens, we believe that sustainable food practices and vegan dishes should be inclusive and accessible to all unique backgrounds and palates. That’s why we’ve curated a remarkable menu of 100% plant-based dishes from around the world, honoring culinary traditions from every continent while also protecting our environment. In addition, our delectable flavors are derived from fresh ingredients ethically grown in our own garden, ensuring every meal nourishes our customers with numerous nutrients.

Fundamentally, through our eco-friendly systems and diverse recipes, Global Greens is dedicated to bridging the gap between delicious cuisines and environmentally conscious food decisions.
